How to use Let's Hangout?

Why should you use Let's Hangout?

Our website allows for collaborative planning of events where people can come togehter and use the same code to enter their free slots, and the website provides a summary. Use our UI to navigate to the right pages and get the information required!

How do I create and share a code?

On the home page, click on new code, and set up the parameters, and it will generate a text code for you. Share with will all your friends to do the scheduling!

How do I use a code?

If you have an existing code, click on enter code, enter the code, and perform the steps as requested by the website!
